# Overview of sales premium

Sales premium empowers sellers to achieve more to align with today's dynamic and unpredictable environments that organizations operate in. Sales premium helps sellers to:    
- Become more productive and spend time on valuable activities
- Build stronger relationships with their customers
- Stay on top of their pipeline    
- 
Sales premium collects data from Dynamics 365 and the entire Microsoft 365 eco-system, and with the power of AI provides insights and recommendations handed in a delightful experience to your sellers.

<!-- [Overview premium video] -->	

Sales Premium offers a comprehensive set of advanced capabilities that are tied together to help your sellers achieve their goals successfully.
- [Accelerate sales](#accelerate-sales)   
- [Build relationships](#build-relationships)   
- [Optimize outcome](#optimize-outcome)   
## Accelerate sales
Empower digital sellers to spend time on the right deals by organizing their work and communicate with their customers more effectively, so sellers can increase their productivity, prioritize their activities for the day, and close.    
<!-- [Sales Accelerator overview video] -->

Customers can personalize workspaces for their sellers to organize and prioritize their work for the day intuitively.    
Customers in high velocity industries often need to assign large number of leads and opportunities to the right sellers at speed, with engagement rules, you can create automatic intelligent rules, so each record get directly to the right seller. AI-driven models ensure that these rules are continually optimized based on historical behavior. <!-- assignment rules link -->    

Once records are assigned to sellers, customer can maximize the potential of leads to qualify or opportunities to win by guiding their sellers with best practices. With sequences, customers can define a sequence of activities for sellers to follow to maximize the outcome. Managers continually monitor and receive suggestions how to optimize the guidance. More information: [Manage sequences](create-manage-sequences.md).   
With premium, companies can also add intelligence into the sellers daily workspace with predictive scoring that predicts the likelihood for leads to qualify and opportunity to win based on learning models. Predictive scoring guides sellers to focus on the right leads and opportunities. More information: [Predictive lead scoring](configure-predictive-lead-scoring.md) and [Predictive opportunity scoring](configure-predictive-opportunity-scoring.md).    
Premium also helps to be more productive when engaging with customers. It guides sellers during and after customer calls with key moments and insights from the call and with follow-up recommendations as a call ends, so they never miss. More information: [Overview of Conversation Intelligence](dynamics365-sales-insights-app.md).    

## Build relationships    
Help sellers build stronger, long-term relationships with their customers with tools to get complete visibility on the quality and trend of their relationships. More information: [View customer activity history](relationship-analytics.md), [Know conversation starters for your customers](talking-points.md), and [Get introduced to lead](who-knows-whom.md).

## Optimize outcome    
Helps sales organizations drive predictability and accountability in their revenue generating activities by providing actionable insights, consistency in execution, and accurate forecasting. More information: [Analyze revenue outcome using predictive forecasting](analyze-revenue-outcome-using-predictive-forecasting.md).

# Find data in a view with AI in Dynamics 365 Sales

You can find, filter, and sort data in views using natural language queries instead of applying complicated filter criteria. 


## Example usage

**Example 1:**

You're in the All Accounts view and want to find accounts in New York with revenue greater than $1 million. 
Search for: "Accounts in New York with revenue greater than $1 million"

**Example 2:**

You're in the Opportunities view and want to see all open opportunities from accounts in the technology industry with estimated close dates in the next quarter.
Search for: "Opportunities from accounts in the Technology industry, with estimated close dates in the next quarter"

**Example 3:**

You're in the My Open Leads view and want to find leads from a specific marketing campaign that were created last month.
Search for: "Leads from Summer Campaign created last month"

# Configure and access voicemail for the Sales Hub dialer (preview)

[!INCLUDE [public-preview-banner](../includes/public-preview-banner.md)]

Calls that can't be assigned to a seller — due to capacity limits, configuration issues, or because the seller rejects or doesn't answer — automatically route to voicemail. The caller hears a prompt to leave a message.

For voicemail configuration options, see [Configure voicemail](/dynamics365/customer-service/administer/voice-channel-voicemail).

[!INCLUDE [public-preview-note](../includes/public-preview-note.md)]

## Access voicemail for a known record

If the voicemail is linked to a known lead, contact, or account:

1. Open the record in **Sales Hub**.
1. Go to the **Activities** section.
1. Use the **Activity Type** filter to display voicemails.

## Access all voicemails

To view all voicemails regardless of record association:

1. In **Sales Hub**, select **Activities** from the site map.
1. On the **Activities** page, use the **Activity Type** filter to show voicemails.

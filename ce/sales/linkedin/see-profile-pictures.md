# Show LinkedIn profile pictures for matched leads and contacts

For leads and contacts that match a LinkedIn member profile, the system can show profile pictures from LinkedIn. 

1. In the sales app, go to **Settings** > **Advanced Settings**.
1. On **Business Management**, select **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**.
1. Turn on the **Daily profile picture updates** toggle and select **Save**.

    The system now displays profile pictures for matched leads and contacts.

To enable the display of the profile image in the contact or lead form, you must enable **Sales Navigator CRM sync** in the LinkedIn Sales Navigator settings. For more information about how to enable CRM sync, see [Integration between Sales Navigator and your CRM](https://www.linkedin.com/help/sales-navigator/answer/82207/integration-between-sales-navigator-and-your-crm-overview).  

If you can't see the profile picture in custom forms, perform the steps as defined in the [Unable to view photos on custom contact and lead forms](/troubleshoot/dynamics-365/sales/lisn-unable-view-photos-custom-contact-lead-forms) section.  

> [!NOTE]
>
> - You can only capture profile pictures for matched leads and contacts from LinkedIn.
> - The profile picture from LinkedIn for a matched contact or lead only appears in the out-of-the-box Contact or Lead form in the apps built on Unified Interface.

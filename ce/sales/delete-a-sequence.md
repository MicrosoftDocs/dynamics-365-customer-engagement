# Delete a sequence 

You can delete sequences that your organization no longer needs. When you delete a sequence, the app removes it permanently. You can delete active or inactive sequences. When you delete an active sequence, the app disconnects the records that the sequence connected.

## To delete a sequence

1. Sign in to your sales app.   
1. Go to **Change area** in the lower-left corner of the page and select **Sales Insights settings**.   
1. Under **Sales accelerator**, select **Sequences**.        
1. On the **Sequences** page, hover over the sequence you want to delete, and then select **More options** > **Delete**.  
1. In the confirmation message, select **Delete permanently**.      
    
    >[!NOTE]
    >If you delete a sequence that's in the active state, the app displays a confirmation message that the sequence is deactivated and deleted. Select **Deactivate and delete**.

# Work with LinkedIn activities in sequences

Use LinkedIn-related activities that are defined for sequences in the sales accelerator to display steps in the work list and **Up next** widget.

## What are LinkedIn activities?

As a seller, use the LinkedIn network, which contains millions of users and offers a unique depth and quality of information that can help you research and connect with your customers. This functionality allows you to use LinkedIn Sales Navigator in your sequence to recommend the next best action.

When a sequence contains steps that are related to LinkedIn activities, you can perform the following actions to interact with customers:

-  Research a customer to learn more about their preferences. For more information, see [Research activity](#research-activity).
- Get introduced to a customer. For more information, see [Get introduced activity](#get-introduced-activity).
- Send personal messages to connect with a customer. For more information, see [Connect activity](#connect-activity).
- Send an InMail to a customer. For more information, see [Send InMail activity](#send-inmail-activity).

## Send InMail activity

Use the **Send InMail** activity to directly message customers who you're not already connected to on LinkedIn.

You can perform the actions described in the following table.

|   &nbsp;     | Up next widget | Work list item |
|--------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Sample image** | :::image type="content" source="media/sa-linkedin-activity-send-inmail-step.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Send InMail activity step in the Up next widget."::: | :::image type="content" source="media/sa-linkedin-activity-send-inmail-step-work-list.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Send InMail activity step in a work list."::: |

> [!NOTE]
> To view the profile of the customer, select **Show profile**.

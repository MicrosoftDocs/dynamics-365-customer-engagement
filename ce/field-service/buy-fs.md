# Buy Dynamics 365 Field Service licenses

Purchase a Dynamics 365 Field Service subscription and assign it to users.

If you're evaluating the product, start with [a free 30-day trial](trial-signup.md) (no admin privileges required).

## Prerequisites

- You have a [Microsoft 365 administrator account](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/business/office-365-administration). If you can't access the admin center, contact your Dynamics 365 administrator.
- Confirm your region is supported in [Datacenter regions](/power-platform/admin/new-datacenter-regions).
- Decide which license to buy: [Available Field Service license types](#available-field-service-license-types).

## Available Field Service license types

Two types of licenses are available for Field Service:

- The standard Dynamics 365 Field Service license that includes all core features and capabilities.
- The Dynamics 365 Field Service Contractor license that includes a subset of the core features and capabilities for third-party technicians.

For feature comparisons and pricing information, see [Field Service pricing](https://www.microsoft.com/dynamics-365/products/field-service/pricing). For detailed licensing information, see [Dynamics 365 licensing guide](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=866544).

## Buy a Field Service license

1. Sign in to the [Microsoft 365 admin center](https://admin.microsoft.com/AdminPortal/Home) with your organization's admin account.

1. Under **Billing**, select **Licenses**. Search for **Dynamics 365 Field Service**.

1. Purchase the number of licenses needed for your users.

## Add users and assign licenses

After you buy licenses, assign them to users or groups, and then grant the required security roles so users can access Field Service apps. Learn more in: [Set up users, licenses, and security roles](users-licenses-permissions.md).

# Scan a barcode in the mobile app

Use the Dynamics 365 Field Service mobile app to scan a barcode to search for records or simplify data entry.

## Scan a barcode to search for a record

Scan a barcode to search the database for records that match the barcode.

> [!NOTE]
> Global search with barcode scanning isn't available on tablet and Windows versions of the app.

1. In the Field Service mobile app, select the **Search** icon.

1. Select the barcode icon. The camera opens and is ready to scan.

1. Scan a barcode or QR code.

1. If a record matches the scanned barcode, the result displays.

:::image type="content" source="../media/mobile-scan-barcode-new.png" alt-text="Four mobile devices in different stages of the barcode scan process.":::

## Scan a barcode to populate a field value

In the mobile app, a barcode-enabled field has a barcode icon. To populate the field, select the barcode icon. For example, scan a barcode to populate the **Asset Tag** field.

1. In the mobile app, open the page with the barcode-enabled field. In this example, select the **Asset** page and select **New**. Scroll to the **Asset Tag** field.

1. Select the barcode icon. The camera opens and is ready to scan. Scan a barcode or QR code.

   :::image type="content" source="../media/mobile-asset-barcode-icon.png" alt-text="Screenshot for adding a new asset with the barcode icon highlighted.":::

1. After a successful scan, the barcode value is automatically added to the field.

1. Select **Save**.

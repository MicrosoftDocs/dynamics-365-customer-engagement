# Launch the schedule assistant

The [schedule assistant](schedule-assistant.md) recommends resources that match criteria like availability, skills, and location. You can launch it from multiple places, depending on your workflow.

## Launch from the schedule board

Open the requirements pane on the [schedule board](work-with-schedule-board.md), choose a requirement, and select **Find availability**. Using the schedule assistant on the schedule board lets you [configure your schedule board](schedule-board-tab-settings.md) view to your needs.

## Launch from the Book button

Select the **Book** option from one of the following areas:

- The record form of a [scheduling-enabled entity](schedule-new-entity.md). The schedule assistant uses that entity's associated time zone. You can view or change the time zone by opening the entity and selecting **Modify Calendar** in the command bar.
- The *resource requirement* form.
- The *[requirement group](multi-resource-scheduling-requirement-groups.md)* form.
- The list view of a [scheduling-enabled entity](schedule-new-entity.md), after selecting a record.

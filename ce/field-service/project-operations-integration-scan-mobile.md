# Scan a serial or batch number in the mobile app

If your work order product has a serial number, batch number, or both, you can scan it by using your mobile device in the Field Service mobile app rather than entering it manually.

1. Open an existing work order.

1. Open the work order product.

1. Don't make any changes to the form.

   - If the work order product has a batch number, select **More** > **Scan Batch**. Scan the batch number barcode and then save.

     :::image type="content" source="media/project-operations-field-service-mobile-scan.png" alt-text="Screenshot in the mobile app showing the Scan Batch and Scan Serial buttons.":::

   - If the work order product has a serial number, select **More** > **Scan Serial**. Scan the serial number barcode.

1. Make any other changes and then save.
